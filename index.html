A timeline of Freddie's Life

  • 1946 - Farrokh Bulsara was born.
  • 1958 - Farrokh and his friends started a rock group called 'The Hectics'.
  • 1964 - The family moves to London.
  • 1969 - Met Mary Austin, the Love of His Life.
  • 1970 - Queen was formed along with Brian May, Roger Taylor and John Deacon.
  • 1972 - He changes his name to Freddie Mercury.
  • 1973 - The band released their first album called 'Queen' which became popular overnight.
  • 1985 - During Queen's show in Live Aid, the 75-thousand crowd felt euphoric, and Freddie became a rock legend.
  • 1989 - Rumors regarding HIV/AIDS confirmed.
  • 1991 - Freddie died of bronchopneumonia caused by HIV infection.
